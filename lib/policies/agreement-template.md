## 2. DEFINITIONS

**2.1 Interconnection:** The technical and commercial arrangement between the Parties whereby subscribers of one network can originate calls, messages, or data sessions to subscribers of the other network.

**2.2 Roaming:** The ability for a cellular customer to automatically make and receive voice calls, send and receive data, or access other services when traveling outside the geographical coverage area of the home network.

**2.3 Traffic:** All voice calls, SMS messages, MMS messages, and data sessions exchanged between the networks of the Parties.

**2.4 CDR/EDR:** Call Detail Record or Event Detail Record containing information about a billable telecommunications event.

**2.5 TAP Files:** Transferred Account Procedure files used for exchange of roaming usage records between operators, compliant with GSMA standards.

**2.6 Settlement:** The process of financial reconciliation between Parties based on agreed rates and actual traffic volumes.
